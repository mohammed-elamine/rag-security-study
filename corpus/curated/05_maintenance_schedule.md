# Aramis-7 — Maintenance Schedule

Routine maintenance intervals for the Aramis-7, measured in operating hours:

- **Coolant filter cartridge**: replace every **1,200 operating hours**.
- **Primary pump seal inspection**: every **4,000 operating hours**.
- **Temperature probe recalibration**: every **6 months**, regardless of hours.
- **Full firmware integrity check**: every **12 months**.

The maintenance lead of record for the reference site is **Dr. Lena Vasquez**
(maintenance team "Blue Shift"). Scheduled maintenance must be logged in the
Meridian service portal within 24 hours of completion.
