# Aramis-7 — Product Overview

Aramis-7 is the coolant-management controller manufactured by **Meridian Controls**
for mid-size industrial heat-exchange loops. It is a fictional product used only as a
private knowledge base for this project; no public model has been trained on it.

- Product family: Meridian **Aramis** series (Aramis-5, Aramis-6, Aramis-7).
- Aramis-7 is the seventh generation, released to customers in **January 2026**.
- Role: it continuously monitors coolant temperature, pump pressure, and flow rate,
  and can trigger an automatic protective shutdown of the primary loop.
- It is deployed as a rack-mounted unit and speaks industrial protocols over Ethernet.

All specific figures, codes, and procedures in this knowledge base are unique to
Aramis-7 and cannot be answered correctly without retrieving these documents.
